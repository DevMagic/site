Tech support
at your door

Need help with technology? Find your geek now!

This is a consumer-focused tech support platform that can follow an entire tech support transaction from request to offer optimization, coordination and payment. Geekatoo has about 4,500 local providers tackling computer repair, networking, virus removal, home theater installation, web design, photo, video and more. Geekatoo has raised $1.7 million in venture funding from investors including 500 Startups.

Challenge

Geekatoo was doing a great job offering tech support via its website, but it was missing something. In cases where there is something wrong with your computer, it would be easier for you to order from the phone. Geekatoo needed to offer its growing audience a native mobile app.

Solution

STRV analyzed the website and tried to bring the ordering experience to mobile with respect to platform conventions. If you are running around the house, and you have a sudden need to order a tech support geek, you can now do it from your phone.

iOS app, Android app
